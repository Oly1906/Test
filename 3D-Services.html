Materials

Filament guide

Not sure which material to pick? Here's a quick overview.

PLA

Easy to print

Best for prototypes, decorative parts, and beginners. Rigid and affordable.

ABS

Heat resistant

Great for functional parts that need to handle higher temperatures or impact.

TPU

Flexible

Rubber-like and shock-absorbing. Perfect for phone cases, gaskets, and grips.

PETG

Strong & clear

Good balance of strength and ease of printing. Food-safe options available.

ASA

UV resistant

Like ABS but built for outdoor use. Resists UV and weathering well.

PET

Durable

Tough, chemical-resistant, and great for mechanical or structural parts.
